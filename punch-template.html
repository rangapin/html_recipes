Punch Planteur

Making its way to America from Jamaica, this cocktail’s recipe was inexplicably written in verse in every magazine that published the drink. Here’s the recipe from the Kansas Star in 1903: “One of sour, one of sweet, two of strong, and one of weak.”

prep time

10 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

The drink is shaken and served over crushed ice in a Collins glass

step 2

Finished off with a dash of soda water and a mint sprig.

ingredients

3 ounces of dark rum

3/4 ounces of fresh lime juice

1 ounce of simple syrup

1 spoonful of grenadine
