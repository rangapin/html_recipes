prep time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Squeeze lime wedge into a highball glass; drop lime into the glass. Add sugar; muddle. Add ice.

step 2

Gently press mint or slap mint; add to glass. Pour rum and club soda into glass; stir. Garnish with lime slice.

step 3

Tequila Mojito: Substitute silver tequila (such as Jose Cuervo Clasico) for the rum.

ingredients

2 lime wedges

3/4 to 1 cup ice cubes

2 teaspoons sugar

2 ounces light rum

1/2 cup club soda, chilled
