prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Fill a shaker three-fourths full with ice. Place remaining ice in a rocks glass; set aside.

step 2

Add the rum, Triple Sec, juices and amaretto to shaker; cover and shake for 10-15 seconds or until condensation forms on outside of shaker. Strain into prepared glass. Garnish as desired.

ingredients

1-1/2 to 2 cups ice cubes

2 ounces light rum

3/4 ounce Triple Sec

1-1/2 teaspoons lime juice

1-1/2 teaspoons amaretto

Optional garnish: Lime slice, lime twist, edible flowers and fresh pineapple
