Rum Arrange

It has long been a tradition in the West Indies and Réunion Island to flavour white rhum with aromatics producing the famous "rhum arrangé" whose exact ingredients vary according to where you are, the plants available and the "humeur du moment". Our original mix is a blend of exotic leaves with a dominant citrus flavour. Leave the mixture to macerate for at least three weeks in a litre of white rum, then strain.

prep time

6 months.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Get a good rum

step 2

Drop some fruits in it

step 3

Leave it there for a long time and then enjoy!

ingredients

1 L of Rum

1 Kg of Letchis or Lime or Ginger etc.
