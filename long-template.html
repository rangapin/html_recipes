Long Island

There's nothing like treating yourself to a cocktail by the pool on a sweltering day. Our pick of the season? The Long Island iced tea!

prep time

30 min.

cook time

15 min.

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

For a single serving of this tasty drink, place the ice in a Collins or highball glass. Pour all ingredients except the cola into the glass.

step 2

Top the drink off with some cola and give it a quick stir with a barspoon. Garnish with lemon, if desired.

ingredients

1 to 1-1/4 cups ice cubes

1 ounce light rum, like Bacardi

1 ounce Triple Sec
